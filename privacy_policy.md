# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

## Introduction

FreePlayer ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of its users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use and the content you access.
- **Advertising Data**: We use Google AdMob to display ads in our App. AdMob may collect information about your device and usage to provide personalized ads. Please see Google's Privacy Policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Information You Provide

Our App allows you to access media files stored on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit these files to our servers. All media content remains on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

1. **To Provide and Improve the App**: We use device and usage data to improve the performance and functionality of the App.
2. **To Display Ads**: We use advertising data to display relevant ads through Google AdMob.
3. **To Comply with Legal Requirements**: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

## Third-Party Services

Our App uses third-party services that may collect information about you:

- **Google AdMob**: For displaying ads. Please see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Google Play Services**: For app functionality and analytics.

## Data Storage and Security

All media files accessed through the App remain on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information we collect, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.
